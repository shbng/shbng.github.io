I need to learn to simply appreciate art, rather than very often craving to make it! 

When I see Web-Dev art, 3d art, and many other digital yet critical kind of art, my heart sinks a little for some reason. I think it's coming from the place of wanting to make such an art, but not having the space/time/skills to do it. Perhaps some time when I have better work boundaries, I do it. 

But the point is I don't have to always indulge in making/immitating those works. I want to simply admire it for its existence and not feel too moved to make it myself.    

I need to learn to see art from a distance. Maintain some boundaries!
