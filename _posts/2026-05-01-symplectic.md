Last weekend, I was already on a break from studying. I needed it. After trying to work a couple of days after that, I once again find myself pausing it. In the last two days, I took a *break*. I took a break to energise myself with some academic motivation. It's very linear sometimes, just reading reading, checking off section by section, chapter by chapter, without any sense of a medium-term goal. There is a short term goal, and then there's a long-term goal that I can tick off. Now that I know it's going to take a lot of time into the Linear Algebra book, I feel a little unready to face the time.

Actually, this energising and missing some motivation in a grand scheme, is only a small part of the story. I think what happened two days ago was I pushed and used more energy than I can afford into reaching that next section and that next section of problems. Then I used my half-energy brain to solve some. Then I got stuck in that zone of dumbed-down brain mode and not able to solve problems creatively and think with all juices fired. Then that halted the progress... And oh boy, halted progress in this time gives such a negative feedback... So yeah, I needed change of mood, change of juices. Hopefully this blog marks a reset in moods, and I can start the usual study process after this night's sleep (it's 1:45 AM now).  

Yeah, hopefully.
