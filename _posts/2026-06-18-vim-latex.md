Ever since I began to write regularly for my master's thesis, I am sticking to Gille Castle's LaTeX setup. This includes ever growing snippets (Ultisnips), Zathura PDF viewer, and also integrating InkScape with vim for drawing quick figures. I go to overleaf/TeXstudio if I want to make design changes or some typset corrections - which could use good GUI. I could use a lot more vim keystrokes than those I use currently.    

And in October of 2026, I switched completely to Linux. I am using CachyOS on Asus Zephryus G14 (2020). This is a ready and lightweight distro based on Arch Linux and maintained regularly by the ROG community. Basically, it's optimized for gaming. Installing Debian-based distros is a bit hard on this laptop for some reason, and thanks to Asus-Linux for maintaining cachyOS/Arch Linux documentation.
